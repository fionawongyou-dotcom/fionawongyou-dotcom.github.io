# The New Academic Approach of Chinese Contemporary Art

# Exhibition Statement

_The New Academic Approach of Chinese Contemporary Art_ explores an alternative narrative of Chinese contemporary art through the perspective of artistic research, academy-based knowledge production, and cultural continuity. Rather than focusing on the dominant narratives of political pop, cynical realism, or market-driven globalization, the exhibition examines how contemporary artistic practices have emerged from the intellectual environments of China's art academies.

Bringing together artists from multiple generations, the exhibition investigates how contemporary art can develop through dialogue with historical traditions, philosophical inquiry, material experimentation, and interdisciplinary research. The participating artists engage with painting, sculpture, installation, photography, video, and mixed-media practices, demonstrating diverse approaches to rethinking the relationship between tradition and contemporaneity.

The exhibition proposes the academy not merely as an educational institution, but as a site of artistic production, cultural reflection, and knowledge creation. Through this perspective, Chinese contemporary art is understood as an ongoing process of transformation that continuously negotiates local histories, global conditions, and future possibilities.
