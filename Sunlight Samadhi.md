# Artwork Statement

_Sunlight Samadhi_ is an interactive immersive environment that bridges macro-cosmic orders and micro-perceptual experiences through a reflexive feedback loop between the Sun and the human mind.

Drawing upon the Zen Buddhist concept of Great Perfect Mirror Wisdom (Da Yuan Jing Zhi), the work proposes that perception is conditioned by consciousness. The installation transforms real-time EEG signals into dynamic solar visualizations, allowing participants to experience how different states of awareness reveal different dimensions of reality. Rather than presenting the Sun as a fixed astronomical object, the work treats it as a living mirror of consciousness.

Using artificial intelligence and algorithmic mapping, five neural frequency bands—Delta, Theta, Alpha, Beta, and Gamma—are associated with five observational modalities of the Sun, including radio imaging, H-alpha observation, X-ray corona imaging, ultraviolet chromosphere visualization, and ultraviolet coronal observation. As participants enter deeper states of attention and meditative stability, hidden solar structures gradually emerge, transforming inner awareness into cosmic perception.

By connecting real-time brain activity with astronomical observation, _Sunlight Samadhi_ creates a contemplative dialogue between inner space and outer cosmos. The work reframes meditation as an observable and participatory phenomenon, inviting audiences to experience the universe not as something distant and external, but as a mirror reflecting the dynamics of consciousness itself.
